# Final Exam — sitting instructions

The final is sat at the **end of week 27**, after the standalone timed mock
(`y2026p2q4`, early in the week — see [`../weeks/week27.md`](../weeks/week27.md)).
This file contains no questions: the paper is assembled from four real
Cambridge Tripos questions, one of them sealed.

## The paper

**3 hours · closed book · handwritten · attempt all four questions ·
20 marks each (80 total).**

| Q | Source | Status |
|---|--------|--------|
| **Q1 — compulsory** | `y2026p2q3.pdf` (in `../../cambridge-course/exam_questions/` — deliberately not linked) | **Sealed** — opened for the first time inside the sitting |
| Q2 | [`y2023p2q3.pdf`](../../cambridge-course/exam_questions/y2023p2q3.pdf) | Held back — never sat, never drilled |
| Q3 | [`y2024p2q4.pdf`](../../cambridge-course/exam_questions/y2024p2q4.pdf) | Held back — never sat, never drilled |
| Q4 | [`y2025p2q4.pdf`](../../cambridge-course/exam_questions/y2025p2q4.pdf) | Held back — never sat, never drilled |

> **Sealed material.** `y2026p2q3` and `y2026p2q4` have never been opened or
> inspected by anyone. Do not read, extract, or infer their contents.

> **Held back for revision.** `y2023p2q3`, `y2024p2q4`, `y2025p2q4` are
> deliberately unspent until week 27.

**Opening the sealed paper is sitting it.** `y2026p2q3` is the only genuinely
unseen-by-everyone question you will ever get from this course; its value is
spent the moment the PDF is rendered. Do not open it to "check the topic", do
not extract its text, do not let any tool summarise it. Open it when the clock
is running, and not before. The same applies to `y2026p2q4` and the mock
earlier in the week.

## Conduct

1. **Set up first.** Print or queue the four PDFs *by filename only* — Q2–Q4
   were catalogued but never assigned, so resist reading them early too; the
   final should be as unseen as it can be. Paper, pen, a visible clock,
   3 hours clear. No books, no notes, no calculator.
2. **Timing.** Four 20-mark questions at the course's 35-minutes-per-question
   convention is 140 minutes of question time, leaving ~40 minutes for
   reading, checking, and the compulsory question's inevitable surprises.
   Q1 is compulsory and unseen — do not let it eat the paper; if it stalls,
   bank the other sixty marks and return.
3. **Show working.** As on every paper in this course: method carries most of
   the marks; state any convention a trace depends on.

## Marking protocol

Mark the script **the next day, cold** — same-day marking is too generous.

- **Q2–Q4:** mark against [`final-solutions.md`](final-solutions.md)
  (model answers and mark schemes for the three held-back questions). Do not
  open that file until the paper is sat.
- **Q1:** no solutions exist — nobody has read the question. Mark it yourself
  against the syllabus: reconstruct a mark scheme from the question's own part
  weights (Tripos questions print per-part marks), your sheet solutions for
  the topic it lands on, and the per-part marks printed on neighbouring
  years' papers. Be examiner-severe: one distinct made point per mark.
- Score each question out of 20 and the paper out of 80. **Anything under
  ~60% on a question names your last revision target** — re-work the relevant
  sheets before the course closes, per
  [`../weeks/week27.md`](../weeks/week27.md).

This structure spends every reserved question exactly once: one sealed paper
as the mock, one as the unseen compulsory question, and all three held-back
papers as the rest of an unseen final.
